I am Squidge the Syrian hamster. I am around 2.5 years old. This site will chronicle my life and adventures.

I am mischievous and enjoy keeping my owners on their toes! Who knows what I'll get up to next?

My interests include:

  • Sunflower seeds
  • Waking Aleesha up by loudly drinking water
  • Carrots
  • Running on my wheel all night
  • Snatching my favorite treats out of Aleesha's hand
  • Escaping from my cage when Aleesha forgets to close the lid

My daily routine is:

  1. Wake up at 11pm
  2. Drink some water
  3. Dig in my sandpit
  4. Play with Aleesha
  5. Run around the living room
  6. Stuff my cheeks full of treats
  7. Bury my treats in my nest
  8. Run on my wheel until I get tired
  9. Go back to sleep
